## What Do Users Actually Say?

This is where things get nuanced, and I'm not going to sugarcoat it.

The reviews on Trustpilot are all over the place. Some people have been running VPS instances with ServerHost for two or three years without issues. Others have reported slow network speeds that don't match advertised specs, unresponsive support, and a no-refund policy that kicks in the moment a service is activated (they offer a 72-hour money-back window, but it has conditions).

The complaints that come up most:
- **Network speeds** — some users report getting nowhere near the advertised 1Gbps; actual speeds on cheap tiers can vary a lot
- **No refund after activation** — standard policy, but worth knowing going in
- **Support responsiveness** — hits or misses depending on the ticket

The positive side: longtime customers tend to stick around. The LowEndBox community has mixed-to-positive sentiment — people who buy the LowEndBox-exclusive promos and use the VPS for lightweight stuff (personal projects, VPNs, bots, test environments) generally don't have complaints. Problems show up more when people buy cheap plans expecting premium-tier performance.

Bottom line: for non-critical workloads and budget labs, the price-to-specs ratio is hard to beat. For anything mission-critical, do your own benchmarking before committing.
